You'll be remembered

It is always harder to be left behind than to be the one to go. Farewell has a sweet sound of reluctance. Good-bye is short and final, a word with teeth sharp to bite through the string that ties past to the future. Yet, the farewell is not final but only till we meet again. You will move on, live your life. So will we. We will be okay. Someday, in the distant future, we will find each other again.
